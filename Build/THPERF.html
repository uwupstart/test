THPERF Testimonials

"Waterloo’s theatre program is giving me the tools I need to be an independent artist"

Mira J. Henderson
Director of Things We Lost in the Aftermath

"Waterloo's theater program taught me not only about the knowledge I need in the future but also the values."

Wen Qing
Dramaturgy of upstart

"It is never too late to follow your dreams. I am a returning student who has a career in Education and I run my own theatre company. I've returned to the program to improve my craft. I have learned a plethora of knowledge from the program thus far and look forward to continuing my journey."

Lynn Scott
Agnes in Bitter/Sweet Fools

"Not only is my name in the word theatre, but it literally changed my life. As a child, I struggled with extreme anxiety, so much that I could not go upstairs by myself long after the average child. However, my drama experience taught me to challenge that fear and drew me into a world I now love. Because of theatre in homeschool and high school, I am able to do things I never dreamed of being able to do, such as being here in university. Theatre teaches you vital skills that you need for any life path: confidence, willingness to take risks, emotional intelligence, and so on. I am only in my first year, but I have already learned so much and felt so welcomed in the theatre community here at UW, and I know you will too. :)"

Thea Andres (they/them)
Tom in Bitter/Sweet Fools

"I am an exchange student here at the University of Waterloo from Australia. I must say that I have never experienced a theatre program quite like the one here in Waterloo and I am amazed by its professionalism and ability to encourage constant growth in each individual. Since I was young, I always knew that performing was my outlet and so I have done a lot of drama and music over the years. It always inspired me to see the world in new ways and I always felt that I could be myself whilst playing another person! I have turned my passion for theatre and performance into a teaching area alongside English and expect to become a high school teacher in 2022. So far, Waterloo has provided me with an extremely insightful and engaging experience that I will take with me into the classroom. "

Lilly McDonald
Elizabeth in Bitter/Sweet Fools.

"I originally started university at a school in Toronto; I thought I needed to get away from the town I grew up in to grow as a person and as an artist. I ended up back at the University of Waterloo thinking I was settling for my second choice - I was wrong. This program challenges me in ways I didn’t expect, and I appreciate each semester more than the last. It has been a surprising and enriching experience to be taught acting techniques, technical skills, history, and more in order to become a well-rounded theatre practitioner. But by far, the dedication, patience, and wealth of experience that comes from the staff in this program while I have been learning new hands-on skills has proved itself to be the best asset to this program. "

Monica Durlak
Director of Bitter/Sweet Fools

"I came to UWaterloo with the hope of learning basic backstage knowledge. UWaterloo’s theatre program has given me such an immense amount of opportunities for so many aspects of theatre. "

Kristina Nasato
Festival Dramaturg/Engagement Space Designer

"I came to U Waterloo because it's surrounded by a rich and established theatre community and because of its proximity to Toronto. I soon discovered that the theatre program here is equally filled with amazing and talented artists that encourage and inspire everyone to do their best. I can get the skills I need in a supportive and enjoyable environment. "

Emma-Leigh Simonot
Alana in Things we Lost in the Aftermath

"When deciding which University to go to I went back and forth between programs in Toronto and the Theatre and Performance program at the University of Waterloo. I ultimately chose this program because of the Co-Op element that I have paired with my degree. I have gained both hands on experience in class and in the world outside of the classroom. With each semester I can feel myself growing as an artist and becoming a better person. This program is filled with wonderful students and instructors who genuinely care about making Theatre with meaning beyond simple entertainment."

Alexandra Kale
Wardrobe Crew Member
